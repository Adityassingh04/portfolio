About Me

Hi, I'm Aditya Singh. I am a passionate and ambitious individual with a deep interest in computer science and technology. At 19 years old, hailing from the vibrant state of Uttar Pradesh, I am currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at Vellore Institute of Technology. My academic journey began at DPSGI in Uttar Pradesh, where I successfully completed my 10th and 12th grades, achieving remarkable scores. Besides my academic pursuits, I am an avid sports enthusiast, having represented Uttar Pradesh at the Nationals U-16 Tournament and excelled in various disciplines like basketball, cricket, and kho-kho. I also find joy in the world of video games, exploring both first-person shooters like Counter-Strike and PUBG, as well as the immersive open-world experiences offered by games like GTA. Solving puzzles and engaging with Lego constructions are among my favorite pastimes. I am constantly eager to expand my knowledge, and my journey includes valuable internships at Microsoft and Dell, where I gained practical insights into the tech industry. In addition to my academic and extracurricular pursuits, I have worked on exciting projects, including a face detector, disaster management system, and drone development. My diverse skill set includes proficiency in languages such as Python, C, C++, Java, and HTML.
